### Our candidate: Scrum Master ---
#### Why the Scrum Master? - Very misunderstood - Must encourage team engagement - Working with many personalities - Service to team - Help understand and apply Agile Values Note: compare to traditional interviews
http://modernagile.org
#### Qualities to seek in a Scrum Master - Empowers a self-organizing team - Explains *WHY* scrum works - Observes many things simultaneously - Solicits feedback - Not afraid to experiment or fail
#### Even more Scrum Master qualities - Leads by example - Cultivates a learning environment - Allows teams to grow organically - Master of complex environments - Receives feedback well Note: Shu Ha Ri

### Game 1 - Model Behavior ---

OBJECTIVE: Describe a past situation

MATERIALS: modeling clay

CAST: Candidate, 2 interviewers, others observers

Candidate /
Interviewers		Construct a model of the situation and tell the story with interaction from interviewers.

### Game 2 - What's Your Take? ---

OBJECTIVE: Candidate places cards containing quotes from a team space to indicate their position
  1. Immediate attention
  2. Obligation or some attention when needed
  3. No attention

MATERIALS: cards, post-it notes, pens

CAST: Candidate, 2 interviewers, others observers

Activities
Candidate
  • Pick a card
  • Reads quote aloud
  • Places the card in the appropriate section
  • Repeat for each card
InterviewersInteract with candidate asking appropriate questions
ObserversWatch the show
Whole tableRetrospective

Card placement Increment

Game Retrospective

https://answergarden.ch/733590
- what kind of facilitation did you notice? - did you learn something? even as an observer? - did any of the cards move positions? - other diffs from traditional interview? - how else can you use this game?
### Final Outcome ---
- Would you hire your candidate? - How was this experience? - Were you engaged even while observing? - Did you learn something? - Could you implement something like this in your organization? - What other roles could you interview with this format?
