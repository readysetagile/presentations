### What is an ATS? ---
Software that streamlines the hiring process
### Why is it needed? ---

  • Hundreds, or thousands, of applicants for 1 position
    •
  • Fake or duplicated resumes
    •
  • Help managers pick "stand out" candidates for interviews
    •
### Why should you care? ---
  • An ATS will not help you
    •
  • You will never know if, when, how or why your resume was rejected
    •
  • An ATS does not care about aesthetics or correctness
    •
  • No two ATS systems are the same
    •
